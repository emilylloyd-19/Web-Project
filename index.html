About Us
Emily opened her bakery in 2018 in downtown Columbia, MO as a relaxing place for people to go and enjoy a variety of pastries and sweets. Her bakery is one of Columbia's finest and most popular places to grab a snack.
Our Hours
Monday-Saturday: 8am-4pm and Closed on Sundays
Menu
You are sure to find something you love at Emily's bakery with the huge variety of sugary sweets, some being gluten and sugar free. Beverages ranging anywhere from lemonade to hot cocoa are included on the menu to go along with your pastries.
